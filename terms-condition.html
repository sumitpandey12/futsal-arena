Cancellation & Rescheduling:




  1. Cancellation and rescheduling 24 hours prior to the game can be 100% refundable after deducting the transaction fees of the booking amount.
  2. Cancellation and rescheduling 12 hours prior to the game can be 80% refundable also including the transaction fees of the booking amount.
  3. Cancellation and rescheduling 06 hours prior to the game can be 50% refundable also including the transaction fees of the booking amount.
  4. Cancellation and rescheduling 03 hours prior to the play time is not entertained and no refund will be available even if the user cancels their play.
  5. Cancellation of a booking online can be done via the Book My Play mobile application or website with the user ID/login details used at the time of booking.
  6. If an online user/player faces a technical issue while cancelling a booking via the Book My Play mobile application or website.
    1. The player must notify the Book My Play Support Team at least 04 hours prior to the play time as mentioned on the app.
    2. Request to cancel a ticket should be sent by email to support@bookmyplay.in along with the player’s Booking ID and mobile number at least 04 hours prior to the play time as mentioned in the ticket.
  7. Cancellation charges are applicable on actual Booking amount only; service/convenience fees are not refundable. Non-refundable service charge of INR 100 will apply to all booking cancellations.
  8. Bookings are non-refundable if cancellations are requested within 03 hours of play time.
  9. Change of Arena/ Arena cancellation: In case there is an Arena change due to any technical or physical reasons, Book My Play will refund the differential amount to the player upon being intimated via email to support@bookmyplay.in within 7 working days.
  10. In case a user is unable to reach the arena at the given time on the date of play and wishes to change the time/date of play, a reschedule request can be sent to support@bookmyplay.in at least 04 hours prior to the time of the play. Booking fare difference, if applicable, shall be borne by the player for rescheduling the same game on a different time/date. Note: Rescheduling is subject to availability of slots open.
  11. Rescheduling a game can be availed only once per booking, if applicable. Once a game is rescheduled it cannot be further cancelled or rescheduled.
  12. Rescheduled games are non-transferrable and non-refundable.



Refund Policy:




  1. Refunds will only be made in INR via the same payment method used while purchasing the ticket after deducting any applicable fee and/or charges.
  2. Book My Play is not liable to issue refunds for bookings done through third-party vendors/brokers and not directly via the official Book My Play mobile application/website.
  3. Refunds for game bookings online via the Book My Play mobile application/website will be processed within 07 working days (excluding public holidays) from the day of cancellation by the player. A player’s bank may take 15-20 days to credit the refund amount to their account or as per their bank policy.
  4. If the refund amount is not credited to a player’s account within 15-20 days from the date of refund, the player should send an email to support@bookmyplay.in along with proof of game booking, cancellation details and bank statement details so they may be assisted.

  1. Each player is allowed to bring whatever is necessary for comfortably playing in the arena their personal items such as futsal bag, futsal shoes, socks etc.
  2. Any personal belongings or items lost in the Futsal Arena premises is not the responsibility of Book My play and should be discussed upon by the player and the Arena Owner themselves.
  3. Players should not carry items such as weapons, firearms, ammunition, drugs, liquor, smuggled goods, inflammable items or any other articles that are prohibited under law.
  4. Book My Play or the respective Futsal Arena Owners reserves the right to deny entry to a player or any individual accompanying the player, if they feel that the individual is intoxicated or feels threatened with their behaviour or if they feel that the said individual is carrying any of the items mentioned above.
  5. Players are not permitted to carry any pet/animal/livestock inside the Futsal premises.
